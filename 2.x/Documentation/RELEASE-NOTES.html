2017.10.31
  • Authentically-encrypted database containers. Optional, of course. The popular cipher algorithms AES and Threefish are included alongside the cryptographic digest algorithm SHA-3.
  • Automatic and periodic removal of cookies.
  • Bookmarks, cookies, downloads, history, and much more. Support windows may be liberated or pinned.
  • Built-in and configurable content blocker with Accept and Block modes of operation.
  • Completely redesigned. Dooble 2.0 is a complete rewrite. Supported on FreeBSD, Linux, Mac OS X, Windows and any other operating system where Qt 5.9.1 is supported.
  • Highly configurable. Dooble offers configurable glimpses into its internal workings.
  • Open source with a BSD license.
  • Private windows.
  • Proxy support. Whether you enjoy a private or public proxy such as Tor, Dooble provides the mechanisms for your privacy.
  • Spell checker. Fifty-four dictionaries of various languages are included! The size of the list varies per operating system.
  • Zero-dependency browser. Save for the requirements imposed on by Qt, Dooble is a zero-dependency browser.
